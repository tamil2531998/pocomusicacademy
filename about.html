POCO MUSIC ACADEMY


DR.BABU KRISHNAN

KALAI SUDARMANI

Poco Music Academy

Dr.Babu Krishnan is a successful Musician who can handle Tabla, Violin, Saxophone, Drums, Guitar, Keyboard, Piano, Veenai and Rhythm Pad. He hails from Vellore District, Tamilnadu. He has completed his basic education in Vellore District, and he has completed B.Sc - C.Abdul Hakeem College. He has spent his childhood in the rural areas of Vellore District where seeds of music sowed in him. He also completed Diploma in Music in Tamilnadu Government Music College, Chennai, where he also Completed his teacher training and received his Gold Medal in the same. Dr.Babu Krishnan has won several honours and awards, These include: Isai Kalaimani ,Ezhisai Mani, Kalai Sudarmani, Kalai Thoongal and Honorary Doctorate - University of New Jerusalem.


Dr.Babu Krishnan has worked and has got an exposure of playing with many legendary music directors in Tamil cinema and he also performed with legendary singers such as “Navarasa Chakravarthi” T.M.Soundararajan, “Padma Vibhushan” Dr.SP Balasubramaniam, “Isaikuyil” Sushila, LR.Eshwari, “Padma Bhushan” Vani Jayaram, “Kalaimaamani “Janaki, “Padma Bhushan”Chithra, “Kalaimaamani”Mano, “Kalaimaamani” Sinivas, “Kalaimaamani”Unnimenon, “Kalaimaamani” Anuradha Sriram. He had performed more than 5000 stage shows with many musical instruments including Tabla, Saxophone, Drums, Rhythm Pad, etc. He also performed in the famous Tv channel Programmes like Sapthaswarangal & Illamai Puthumai in Suntv, Paattuku Paatu, Nejam Marapathillai, Innisai Malai in Kalaingar tv, Kodai Kondattam in Raj tv, Kadhala Kadhala in Jaya tv, Mannisai DD Pothugai. He is also a member in Cine Musicians' Union.

Poco Music Academy

He is a creative and disciplined music teacher who is deeply committed as a music performer and a teacher, who always puts priority to enhance students music skills in vocal and instrumental forms with encouragement. Having a sound knowledge in singing instruction, instrumental instruction, rhythm and musical notation, and music theory from the single source is a boon to any student .Dr.Babu Krishnan, is registered candidate Trinity College london to send the student for the exam from the same Trinity College London, theory and practical.
